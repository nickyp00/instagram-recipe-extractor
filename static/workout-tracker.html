Tired of complicated fitness apps and messy spreadsheets? In this video, I show you how I built a workout tracking system using AI that lets me take quick, unstructured notes at the gym and automatically organizes everything into a database.

The Problem: Paper logs get lost, spreadsheets become messy, and apps require tedious form-filling for every set.

My Solution: Jot down quick notes like "leg day - squats 135x8, 185x6, 225x4" and let AI handle the rest.

What This System Does:

  • Interprets messy workout notes and stores them in a SQL database
  • Creates charts, analytics, and progress tracking on demand
  • Generates AI-powered workout recommendations based on your history
  • Automatically adds new exercises to the database

Tech Stack: Claude AI + Cloudflare D1 (SQLite database) + Claude Projects

This is a live demo showing both the successes and limitations of AI - including moments where it gets things wrong. Perfect for anyone interested in fitness tracking, AI automation, or building practical AI applications.
